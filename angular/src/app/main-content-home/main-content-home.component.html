We value SAFETY, RESPONSIBILITY AND HAVING FUN

Sand Star Services is a general contracting company dedicated to efficient and quality execution of craftsmanship. In addition to fair pricing and timelines that fit your schedule and budget, we aim to support local education efforts.

A portion of our proceeds go to mentoring youth in the community in the trades that are connected to the services that we offer. By extending these opportunities, we will help students realize their full occupational potential without the burden of cumbersome student loans.
